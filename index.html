Meet Mike

Mike started Mike's Plumbing & Drains to give Chicago homeowners a better option than giant franchises and no-show contractors. He's a local guy with 10+ years of hands-on plumbing experience, and he built the business around the kind of service he would want in his own home: show up, do clean work, explain the problem, and fix it the right way.

Today, Mike's Plumbing & Drains stays intentionally small so customers get direct communication, consistent workmanship, and a familiar face — whether it's a leaking pipe, a drain problem, a water heater issue, or an after-hours emergency call.
